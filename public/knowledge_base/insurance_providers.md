# Accepted Insurance Providers

## Major Insurance Providers - In Network

### Blue Cross Blue Shield (BCBS)
- **Status**: In-network
- **All plans accepted**: Yes
- **Pre-authorization**: Not required for standard visits
- **Copay**: Varies by plan (typically $20-$50)
- **Notes**: Most common insurance provider, excellent coverage

### Aetna
- **Status**: In-network
- **Plans accepted**: Most PPO and HMO plans
- **Pre-authorization**: Required for specialist referrals
- **Copay**: Varies by plan
- **Notes**: May require referral from PCP for specialists

### UnitedHealthcare
- **Status**: In-network
- **Plans accepted**: All UHC plans including Medicare Advantage
- **Pre-authorization**: Not required for primary care
- **Copay**: Varies by plan
- **Notes**: Accepts both commercial and Medicare plans

### Cigna
- **Status**: In-network
- **Plans accepted**: PPO, HMO, and POS plans
- **Pre-authorization**: Required for some procedures
- **Copay**: Varies by plan
- **Notes**: Good coverage for preventive care

### Humana
- **Status**: In-network
- **Plans accepted**: Commercial and Medicare Advantage plans
- **Pre-authorization**: May be required
- **Copay**: Varies by plan
- **Notes**: Popular for Medicare patients

## Government Programs

### Medicare
- **Status**: Accepted
- **Parts covered**: Part B (medical), Part D coordination available
- **Pre-authorization**: Required for certain procedures
- **Notes**: We accept Medicare assignment

### Medicaid
- **Status**: Accepted
- **State plans**: Illinois Medicaid accepted
- **Notes**: Eligibility verification required at each visit

## Out-of-Network Providers

For insurance providers not listed above, patients may have out-of-network benefits. Our billing department can:
- Verify out-of-network coverage
- Provide cost estimates
- Submit claims on patient's behalf
- Set up payment plans if needed

## Important Notes

1. **Insurance Verification**: Always verify current coverage before appointments
2. **Pre-authorization**: Some procedures require pre-authorization - billing handles this
3. **Copays**: Due at time of service
4. **Coverage Changes**: Insurance coverage can change - verify at each visit
5. **Billing Questions**: Transfer to billing department at extension 234

## What We DON'T Accept

- Health savings accounts (HSA) as primary insurance (but accept as payment method)
- Discount medical cards (not actual insurance)
- Most vision-only or dental-only plans (we're primary care)

## For Billing Team Transfer

If patient needs detailed coverage verification, copay amounts, deductible information, or claims status, transfer to billing department.
