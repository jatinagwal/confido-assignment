# Confido Health Clinic Information

## Location and Address

**Confido Health Clinic**
123 Medical Plaza Drive, Suite 200
Springfield, IL 62701

### Directions

**From Downtown Springfield:**
- Take Main Street east for 2 miles
- Turn right on Medical Plaza Drive
- Clinic is in the blue building on the left
- Free parking garage available

**From I-55:**
- Take Exit 92 toward Springfield
- Follow signs to Medical District
- Turn left on Medical Plaza Drive
- Look for blue building with Confido Health sign

**Public Transportation:**
- Bus Route 12 stops directly in front of clinic
- Bus runs every 30 minutes on weekdays
- Nearest metro station: Springfield Medical Center (0.5 miles)

## Business Hours

### Regular Hours

- **Monday - Friday**: 8:00 AM - 6:00 PM
- **Saturday**: 9:00 AM - 2:00 PM
- **Sunday**: Closed

The clinic is closed on the following holidays:
- New Year's Day
- Memorial Day
- Independence Day (July 4th)
- Labor Day
- Thanksgiving Day
- Christmas Day

*Note: Modified hours may apply for holiday eves and weekends - call ahead*

### After-Hours Care

For urgent medical needs after hours:
- **Phone**: (555) 123-4567
- **Press Option 9** for on-call provider
- On-call provider available 24/7 for urgent concerns
- Response time: Typically within 30 minutes

For emergencies, always call 911 or go to nearest emergency room.

- We accept most major insurance plans (see insurance_providers.md)

- Financial assistance programs available

**Billing Questions**: Call extension 234 or email billing@confidohealth.com

## Medical Records

### Requesting Records

- Complete release form (available at front desk or online portal)
- Allow 3-5 business days for processing
- Pick up at office or request mailed copies
- Electronic delivery available via patient portal

**Records Department**: (555) 123-4567 ext. 4

## New Patients

We welcome new patients! To establish care:

1. Call to schedule a new patient appointment
2. Arrive 15 minutes early to complete paperwork
3. Bring:
   - Photo ID
   - Insurance card(s)
   - List of current medications
   - Medical history records (if available)

**Online Registration**: Available at confidohealth.com/new-patient

## COVID-19 Safety

## Important Policies

### Appointment Policies

- **Cancellations**: Must cancel 24 hours in advance
- **No-shows**: May incur $50 fee
- **Late arrivals**: May need to reschedule if more than 15 minutes late

### Prescription Refills

- Request refills 48 hours in advance
- Use patient portal for fastest service
- After-hours: Call pharmacy directly

### Test Results

- Available via patient portal within 3-5 days
- Provider will call for abnormal results
- Request paper copies at front desk
